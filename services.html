How it works

Three rivals in. Monday decisions out.

rivalDrop is a simple loop: pick the rivals, watch the public surfaces, filter the noise, send the brief.

1. Pick the watchlist

We start with three Shopify rivals and the surfaces that matter: product pages, collections, pricing, reviews, blogs, ads, and search pages.

2. Capture changes

We collect public changes and customer reactions through the week. Most are boring. Boring gets filtered.

3. Send Monday moves

You get what changed, why it matters, and what to do next: copy, dodge, answer, ignore, or keep watching.

What we do not do.

We do not scrape private accounts, bypass logins, sell client watchlists, or pretend the machine is magic. Public data only. Human-readable output. Clear limits.

We also do not sell vague "AI competitive intelligence." That phrase sounds expensive and hungry. We sell a Monday brief that helps an operator make a better move.

rivalDrop watches public competitor changes and turns them into Monday decisions. Part of Groundlayer LLC.
