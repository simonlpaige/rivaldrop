How it works

The work is watching. The product is the decision.

We track the public surfaces your rivals update, filter out the junk, and package the few changes that might matter this week.

Public surfaces

Product pages, collections, pricing, restocks, review pages, blog posts, ad libraries, and search snippets.

Customer signal

Complaints, praise, repeated questions, feature requests, forum threads, and review patterns that explain why a change matters.

Operator moves

Each brief ends with actions: adjust price, answer an objection, copy a useful structure, ignore a stunt, or keep watching.

Useful beats impressive.

A giant dashboard can make you feel informed and still leave you stuck. rivalDrop is intentionally small: the few rival moves worth knowing before the week gets away from you.
