Every Shopify brand is a bucket with seven holes.

Most competitive intel tools plug one hole. They tell you when a competitor drops a price. That's useful. But your brand is also losing money to quiet restock gaps, missed Reddit threads, competitors you've never heard of, and product categories you're already behind on. One tool doesn't fix a leaky bucket. Seven do.

RivalDrop runs seven analysts in parallel, feeds them into one weekly email, and tells you the three moves worth making this week. Not a dashboard. Not a data dump. A short read with clear actions.

The seven analysts

Each analyst has one job. They run every day. The email surfaces only what moved.

⚡ Price Watch Any price change at a tracked competitor. Caught within 24 hours, with direction and percentage delta. No price drift goes unnoticed for six months.
📦 Bestseller Tracker Which of their products are climbing the rankings, which are falling. The core intel that tells you what's actually selling, not what's being advertised.
🛒 Product Roadmap Analyst Monthly: three product categories your competitors carry that you don't, ranked by momentum. Stop guessing what to build next.
🛑 Restock Pulse When a competitor's top SKU goes out of stock, you get a same-day flag. That's your window to run an ad, bump inventory, or capture the cross-shopper.
⭐ Reputation Radar Watches Reddit, niche forums, Trustpilot, and Google reviews for mentions of your brand AND your five competitors. Catches new threads the day they post.
🔎 SEO Gap Analyst Monthly: ten keywords your competitors rank for that you don't, with search volume attached. A real content roadmap, not a guess.
🆕 New Entrant Scanner Watches marketplaces, Kickstarter, niche discovery platforms, and community launch threads. Flags any new brand gaining traction before they hit your radar the hard way.
