FAQ

A few boring boundaries. Good services have them.

Do you need access to my Shopify admin?

No. The report uses public storefront data only. Do not send passwords, private admin links, or customer data.

What if a competitor is not on Shopify?

We can still review the public site, but those sections may be lighter because the data is less structured.

Is this a recurring plan?

No. One Fiverr order, one report. The premium package includes one 30-day follow-up addendum.

What do I send after ordering?

Your store URL, competitor URLs or names, and any focus area such as pricing, SEO, product gaps, or restocks.
