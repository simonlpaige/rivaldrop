How it works

You tell us who to watch. We watch them. Once a week, we send you a report of everything that changed. If nothing changed, we say so. That's it.

You name competitors

Tell us who to watch. Two competitors, five, ten. Give us their website, their Google listing, their Shopify store. We take it from there.

RivalDrop watches them

We check their pricing pages, Google profiles, Shopify storefronts, and ad copy on a regular cadence. Automated, persistent, and quiet.

You get a weekly report

A short, plain-English email. What changed, who changed it, and when. If something big moves mid-week, we flag it the same day.

What we watch

Pricing pages Price increases, new tiers, discontinued plans, promotional pricing. Tracked with timestamps.
Google Business listings Description changes, new photos, hours updates, review velocity. The public face of a local competitor.
Shopify storefronts New products, removed SKUs, collection changes, theme updates. The catalog tells a story.
Ad copy (where detectable) Changes to Google Ads headlines, new messaging angles, seasonal campaigns appearing or disappearing.
Product catalogs New launches, bundle changes, inventory signals, category expansions. What they sell and how they frame it.
Promotions and sales Discount codes, seasonal offers, flash sales, loyalty programs. When they run them and how deep the cuts go.
