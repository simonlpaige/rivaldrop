FAQ

A few boring boundaries. Good services have them.

Is the report really white-label?

Yes. Your agency name and logo go on the cover and footer. Your client sees your brand. We stay invisible.

Do you need access to the client's Shopify admin?

No. The report uses public storefront data only. Never send passwords, private admin links, or customer data.

Is this a subscription?

No. The pilot is a single $149 one-time payment for one report. There is nothing to cancel and rivalDrop does not store card details; payment is processed by Stripe.

What happens right after I pay?

You land on the intake form. We need your agency name, logo, work email, your client's store URL, up to three competitor URLs, and an optional focus area such as pricing or SEO.

What if a competitor is not on Shopify?

We can still review the public site, but those sections may be lighter because the data is less structured.
