Welcome. Here's what happens next.

Onboarding takes about 5 minutes. You tell us who to watch, we confirm the list, and your first report arrives within 7 days.

  1. You tell us who to watch.

    Name the competitors you care about. Give us their website, Google listing, or Shopify store URL. Two competitors is enough to start. You can add more later.

  2. We confirm the list.

    Simon reviews your competitors and replies within 24 hours to confirm what we'll track and how often.

  3. First report within 7 days.

    Your first weekly report lands in your inbox. After that, reports arrive every week on the same day.

  4. Edit anytime.

    Want to add a competitor, drop one, or change what we focus on? Email simon@rivaldrop.com anytime.
