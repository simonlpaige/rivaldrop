Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

RivalDrop is a service of Groundlayer LLC, a Missouri limited liability company. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use rivaldrop.com and our competitive intelligence services.

What we look at (and what we don't)

We only look at things competitors have already made public: their websites, their Google Business profiles, their Shopify storefronts. We don't scrape private accounts, we don't break anyone's login, we don't do anything you couldn't do yourself with a browser.

Information you give us

  • Your name and email address (when you subscribe, onboard, or contact us)
  • Business information (store URL, competitor names and URLs, provided during onboarding)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe; we never see or store card numbers)

Information collected automatically

This website uses Google Fonts, which means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when loading the page. We do not use cookies, analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or any other third-party tracking.

How we use your information

  • To deliver your weekly competitive intelligence reports
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To respond to your questions
  • To send service-related emails (report delivery, onboarding confirmation, account updates)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

  • Stripe - payment processing
  • Resend - transactional email delivery
  • Google Fonts - web font delivery

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your subscription is active, plus 12 months after cancellation for re-engagement purposes. You can request deletion at any time by emailing simon@rivaldrop.com.

Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you

To exercise these rights, email simon@rivaldrop.com.

Security

Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and never touches our systems. All data in transit uses HTTPS encryption.

Children

Our services are not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Subscribers will be notified of material changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top shows the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy: simon@rivaldrop.com
