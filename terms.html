1. Services

rivalDrop provides one-time, white-label competitor teardown reports for marketing agencies, consultancies, and other businesses that serve ecommerce clients. Each report covers one client store and up to three competitors. Reports are based on public competitor pages, public ads and search signals, visible offer structure, checkout flow observations, and other publicly available business evidence.

rivalDrop distinguishes between observable facts (publicly visible prices, product availability, review counts) and inferences (conclusions drawn from those observations). All material claims in a report include a source note indicating whether the claim is directly observed or inferred.
